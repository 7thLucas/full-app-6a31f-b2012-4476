The Problem & Solution

Pet owners deserve one place for everything.

😓

The Problem

Pet owners today are stuck juggling three or more separate apps just to care for their pets — one for supplies, another to book a vet, and a third to reserve a boarding spot.

  • ✗ No centralised pet profile or medical history
  • ✗ Missed appointments and forgotten reminders
  • ✗ Business owners managing chaos across spreadsheets
  • ✗ No 24/7 support — customers wait to contact staff
🐾

The Solution — MyPet

MyPet is a single platform that unifies every pet care need — shopping, vet scheduling, hotel booking, and a 24/7 AI assistant — alongside a full admin panel for the business.

  • ✓ One app, one login, complete pet profile
  • ✓ Instant reminders for every booking and appointment
  • ✓ Business dashboard — inventory, schedules, revenue
  • ✓ AI Pet Assistant available around the clock
