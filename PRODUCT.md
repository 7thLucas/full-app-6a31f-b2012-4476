# MyPet — Pet Care & Pet Shop Management Platform

## What It Is
A complete digital hub for pet owners and pet shop businesses. Customers can shop for pet products, book veterinary appointments, and reserve pet hotel stays — all in one app. Business owners get a full admin panel to manage everything.

## Target Users
- **Pet Owners (Customers)**: Need a single app to handle all pet-related needs — shopping, vet bookings, boarding
- **Pet Shop Business Owners / Admins**: Need tools to manage products, services, bookings, and revenue

## Core Customer Features
- Browse & purchase pet products (food, toys, accessories, health supplies)
- Search/filter products by pet type, category, brand
- Schedule veterinary appointments; view available vets and services
- Book pet hotel stays (boarding & daycare)
- Manage pet profiles (name, breed, age, medical info)
- View booking history and order history
- Receive reminders for appointments and hotel reservations

## AI Pet Assistant (24/7 Chatbot)
- Answer questions about pet products
- Recommend products based on pet type, breed, age, needs
- Explain veterinary services and procedures
- Help customers book appointments and hotel stays
- Answer FAQs and provide basic pet care tips
- Help track bookings and orders
- Escalate complex questions to staff

## Admin Features
- Manage products and inventory
- Manage veterinary services and schedules
- Manage pet hotel rooms, cages, and availability
- Track bookings and appointments
- View sales and revenue reports
- Manage customer info and pet profiles
- Review chatbot conversations and common questions

## Brand & Tone
- Warm, friendly, modern — appeals to pet owners
- Trustworthy, organized, professional
- Centered around best care for pets
- App name: MyPet
